About: Oil and Water

Explore how polar and non-polar substances interact.

It is well known that "oil and water don’t mix." But why? Run the model of polar water (blue and red) and non-polar oil (gray). "Shake up the oil and water mixture," then run it again and watch what happens to the molecules over time.
